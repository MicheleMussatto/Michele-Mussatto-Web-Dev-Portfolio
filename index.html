I live to to motivate people to action: through my web designs, illustrations, writing, videos, social media and creative marketing. I am handy with a DSLR camera and photo and video editing software. I have a B.S. in Graphic Design, am working on my M.S. in Multimedia Design, and am wrapping up a web dev bootcamp at Kansas City's Tallgrass School. I am seeking full-time employment, but am also busy working on my web dev and marketing business High Sign Design, as well as producing video interviews, concert reviews and concert photography for Unrated Magazine. I work with Atom on Linux, with Dreamweaver on Mac, and with Sublime on Windows. I design illustrations, logos, photos, videos and moving graphics with Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Premiere Pro and After Effects. I also develop with Wordpress, PHP and Bootstrap.
